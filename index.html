About Me

Hi! I'm Chip Carnes, and web development is my passion. I help people build websites that serve their needs, and I love helping make others lives better, whether through technology, business, strengths development, or music.

I believe that developing for the web is an avenue to develop people in technologically advancing nations, and that in an increasingly globalized world, not only should we stewards our advancements for the benefit of others but that we also have much to learn from those interacting with technology in other nations.


After studying music in college, and working with non-profit organizations overseas for a year, I became motivated to find a skill set that would allow me to travel and to pass on to the next generation in developing countries. All of these things culminated upon programming, which I was introduced to shortly after coming back to America.

When I'm not programming, I'm probably working on a Bach sonata on my viola, playing board games with my wife, or reading about international development. Go ahead and scroll down to see some examples of my work.
Have an idea stirring in your head? I'd love to hear from you!
